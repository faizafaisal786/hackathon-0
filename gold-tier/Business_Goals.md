## Financial Metrics & Alerts

### Automatic Alerts (Odoo sync)
- **Overdue > 14 days:** Create P1 task → draft payment reminder
- **Overdue > 30 days:** Create P0 task → escalate to owner
- **New subscription detected:** Log in accounting audit
- **Large unexpected expense:** Flag in CEO briefing
- **Revenue drop > 20% MoM:** P0 alert in daily briefing

### Weekly Accounting Audit Goals
- All invoices reconciled
- Expense categories reviewed
- Cash flow projection updated
- Subscriptions audit (cancel anything not in use)

## Notes for Claude

- **Financial data is sensitive.** Never put actual revenue figures in Dashboard.md — use percentages or ranges. Full figures go in `Accounting/` folder only.
- **Social engagement > follower count.** When evaluating social performance, prioritize engagement rate over raw follower count.
- **Lead quality > quantity.** When reporting leads, note whether they match target customer profile.
- **Cash flow over revenue.** When Odoo data is available, always report cash position first, revenue second.

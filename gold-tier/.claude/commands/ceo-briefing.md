# Skill: CEO Monday Morning Briefing

Generate the weekly CEO briefing every Monday morning.

Steps:
1. Read the latest `/Briefings/*_Weekly_Audit.md`.
2. Read `Business_Goals.md` — compare actuals vs targets.
3. Read `Dashboard.md` for current state.
4. Read `/Accounting/Current_Month.md` for revenue data.
5. Scan `/Done` for key wins this week.
6. Identify top 3 bottlenecks from overdue items.
7. Look for proactive cost-saving opportunities (unused subscriptions, budget overruns).
8. Write `/Briefings/YYYY-MM-DD_Monday_CEO_Briefing.md`:

```markdown
# Monday Morning CEO Briefing — <date>

## Executive Summary
<2-3 sentence business health statement>

## Revenue This Week
- Total: $X,XXX
- MTD: $X,XXX (X% of $XX,XXX target)
- Trend: On Track / Behind / Ahead

## Completed This Week (<count>)
- Key achievements list

## Bottlenecks
| Task | Expected | Actual | Delay |

## Proactive Suggestions
### Cost Optimization
- <specific savings opportunities>
### Upcoming Deadlines
- <critical deadlines in next 14 days>

## Action Items for Today
1. <most critical action>
2. <second priority>
3. <third priority>

---
*Generated by AI Employee — Gold Tier*
```

9. Update `Dashboard.md` with briefing link.
10. Output: `<promise>CEO_BRIEFING_COMPLETE</promise>`
